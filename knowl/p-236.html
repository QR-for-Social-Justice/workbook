Reduce several numbers modulo \(26\text{,}\) including numbers much larger than \(26\text{,}\) using various notions of “congruence modulo \(26\)” (e.g. \(a\equiv b\mod26\) if \(a\text{:}\)00 and \(b\text{:}\)00 are the same time on a \(26\)-hour clock, \(a\) and \(b\) have the same remainder upon division by \(26\text{,}\) \(26\mid (a-b)\text{,}\) subtracting a multiple of \(26\) from \(b\) yields \(a\)).
