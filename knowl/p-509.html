The Coastal Risk Screening Tool 81  by Climate Central allows you to see maps of different cities after sea levels have risen. For example, this map shows the New York City area with 2.2m of sea level rise 82 , the upper limit of the sea level rise estimated in [4.11.1.39]. You can see that large parts of the area are underwater, including Newark and LaGuardia International Airports, Coney Island, and large parts of downtown Manhattan, including the World Trade Center. The densely populated city of Guangzhou, China, would suffer even more under the worst-case scenario of 1.99 m of sea level rise 83 .
