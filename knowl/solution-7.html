Solution 3.8.5.1.

In China, we have
\begin{equation*} \frac{1.05*10^{10}\text{ tons }CO_2}{1.43*10^9 \text{ people}} = 7.34\text{ tons }CO_2/\text{ person} \end{equation*}
In the USA, we have
\begin{equation*} \frac{5.26*10^9\text{ tons }CO_2}{3.29*10^8 \text{ people}} = 15.99\text{ tons }CO_2/\text{ person} \end{equation*}
The USA produces more than twice as much \(CO_2\) per person as China.
