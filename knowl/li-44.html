Item 1.

Controversy and vulnerability: different views are expected and honored with a group commitment to understand the sources of disagreement and to work cooperatively toward common solutions without putting a disproportionate responsibility on marginalized groups to educate us.

  • No one's identities are under question: e.g. no arguing that queer people are wrong for existing.

  • Please come talk to your instructor anytime you feel uncomfortable enough that it's affecting your work in class.
